Rationale

The purpose of this lesson plan is to help high school students become familiar with the FDR, the breadth of the Great Depression, and the short  and long term role of the New Deal programs in American society.  Even today, several New Deal programs are a part of our lives, including Social Security, Labor Unions (Wagner), agricultural business practices, and the current welfare state.  The activities in this lesson serve to promote higher order thinking skills, problem solving skills, group interaction and team work skills, and better writing skills.
