Georgia WPA Murals

If you wish to do a fun, in-depth activity that spreads across several weeks (if not months) and that can incorporate other disciplines, I suggest you locate a mural close to your school and follow the example of Living Schoolbook mural project.

Location of murals:
According to Marlene Park's book, Democratic Vistas (which is out of print) there are murals in Adel, Augusta, Blakely, Cairo, Camilla, Cochran, College Park, Commerce, Conyers, Conelia, Cuthbert, Decatur, Eastman, Gainesville, Greensboro, Hartwell, Jackson, Jesup, Lawrenceville, Louisville, Lyons, McDonough (in the federal building once a post office), McRae (missing), Manchester, Monticello, Pelham, Rockmart, Rome, Summerville, Swainsboro, Sylvania, Sylvester, Vidalia (destroyed), Warrenton, Winder, and Wrightsville.
