    American Student Union Summer Institute Autobiographies (1938, 1939)

    From the Joseph P. Lash Papers, FDR Library

    The following selection of autobiographical essays were written by student activists during the American Student Union's 1938 and 1939 summer training institutes. They can be found in the Joseph P. Lash Papers at the Franklin D. Roosevelt Library in Hyde Park, NY.
