    Robert Cohen and Thomas Thurston wish to dedicate this feature to today's graduate student union organizers.

    Essay and Primary Documents

    Robert Cohen is a professor in NYU's School of Education. His essay "Activist Impulses: Campus Radicalism in the 1930s" is taken from his book, When the Old Left Was Young: Student Radicals and America's First Mass Student Movement, 1929-1941 (New York: Oxford University Press, 1993. Copyright, Robert Cohen).

    The primary documents that make up this feature were selected by Professor Cohen.

    HTML Development and Design

    Thomas Thurston designed the website and led the development of this project.

    Photographs

    In 1940 Rondal Partridge received a commission from the National Youth Administration to document "California Youth." His photo essays constitute an evocative look at Americas youth during the moment in which the country moved from depression to war. Mr. Partridge's photo essays can be found on the New Deal Network at Rondal Partridge, NYA Photographer.
