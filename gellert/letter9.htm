Israel Keller;

Please preserve the historic Gellert Murals. Hugo Gellert is an important American artist, and I would consider the destroying of his work to be a crime against humanity. It would be terrible matter for you, as President of the Board of Seward Park Housing Corporation, to be responsible for the destruction of the murals. They belong to posterity.

Thank you.
