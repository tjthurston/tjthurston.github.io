Dear Sir,

This letter is to urge you to oppose the destruction of the murals of Hugo Gellert in the lobby of the Seward Park Housing Complex. They are part of the artistic heritage of New York and of the United States, and they reflect an important part of the community's history.

Sincerely,

David Suisman

cc: Israel Keller, President, Seward Park Housing Corp; James Wechsler, CUNY Art History Department
